Rules

- For all the games, there will be 2 referees and time/ score keepers
- Both teams shall warm-up simultaneously prior to the game
- A coin flip shall determine which team gets the first possession. The team that wins the coin flip can either choose to benefit from the ball possession at the beginning of the game or at the beginning of a potential overtime
- Every successful shot inside the arc is 1 point
- Every successful shot behind the arc is 2 points
- Every successful free throw is 1 point
- A team is in a penalty situation after it has committed 6 fouls. After a team has reached 9 team fouls, any subsequent foul will be considered technical
- Fouls during the act of shooting inside the arc shall be awarded 1 free throw, whilst fouls during the act of shooting behind the arc shall be awarded 2 free throws
- Fouls during the act of shooting followed by a successful field goal shall be awarded 1 additional free throw
- Team fouls 7, 8, and 9 shall always be awarded 2 free throws. The 10th and any subsequent team foul, as well as technical and unsportsmanlike fouls, will be always awarded 2 free throws and ball possession
*During the game, water will be provided
*Exact time schedule may change depends on the number of teams and field situation

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions
Q: Can I sign up for more than one competition?
A: Of course, you can sign up as many as you want. Our goal for holding the competitions is to let you enjoy what you want and like to do!
Q: How do I pay for the sign-up fee?
A: We use PayPal's personal transfer to charge the sign-up fee. The cost will be different depends on which competition you participate (See GAMES for the sign-up fees)
Q: What is the latest time to turn in the sign-up form?
A: For our convenience to last update the participants, the last day to turn in the form will be a week before the game day. (See GAMES for the dates of each competition)
Q: Can I change the information after I submitted the sign-up form?
A: Yes, you can change it as many time as you want before the deadline. Once the deadline has passed, you are not allowed to make any more changes.
